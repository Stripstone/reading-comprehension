📘 How This Works

Your goal is simple:
Read the passage carefully.
Write a short consolidation of what the passage actually does.
Choose how you want to evaluate it:
  • See how many core anchors you captured, or
  • Get AI feedback and a clarity score.
"If someone only read your consolidation, would they understand how the passage works?"
💡 What Makes a Strong Consolidation?
  • Captures the core idea
  • Finish within the character limit
  • Discover keywords and anchors in text
